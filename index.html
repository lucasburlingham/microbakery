About Us

First - what is a microbakery (official term: Home Based Vendor)? It's a small-scale business that vends foodstuffs directly to consumers within the vendor's state. Vendors are required to comply with local health and safety regulations, and be certified Food Managers. Microbakeries are often run from home kitchens, allowing for lower overhead costs and a more personalized touch to the products offered.
For more information, please visit this link from the Indiana Department of Health summarizing the regulations and requirements for home-based food businesses.
